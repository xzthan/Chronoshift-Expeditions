Hunting the Past

Analyzing the skill set and experience of a sportsman is fundamental to guaranteeing the most thrilling yet safe experience for all those involved in the meticulous process of Hunting the Past. Chronoshift Expeditions offers various packages, most notably the Classic Package by the most frequent patrons. However, the 'Op Package is most recommended for beginning to intermediate hunters. Furthermore, the Horseback Package is offered only to the most experienced hunters.

'Op Package

Starting at $15,000

The ‘Op Package is available to all clients and is best suited for the Cambrian, Ordovician, Silurian, and Devonian eras.

Classic Package

Starting at $10,000

The Classic Package is a favorite of all first time patrons, and it is commonly selected for the Triassic, Jurassic, and Cretaceous period hunts.

Horseback Package

Starting at $20,000

The Horseback Package is available to only second-time patrons during the Paleocene, Eocene, Oligocene, Miocene, and Pliocene eras.
